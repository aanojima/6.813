What is UCHI?

Uchi is a curated source for interior design inspiration. Whether you just moved into your first apartment or you're styling your next house, the designers at Uchi can help you find the design ideas that are right for you.

Where do I start?

To get started, simply select the room you want to design above and hit Go - you'll instantly see many ideas that you can filter based on multiple criteria. You don't need an account, but if you want to save your favorite designs and remember past searches, you can sign up for free!
